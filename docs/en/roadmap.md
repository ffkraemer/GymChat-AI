# Roadmap

## Purpose

GymChat AI will be developed incrementally, reducing technical and business risks through progressive value delivery.

The primary focus of the platform is member loyalty and engagement through WhatsApp and Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Phase 1 — POC (Proof of Concept)

## What is it?

A Proof of Concept designed to validate the technical feasibility of the solution.

## Objectives

Validate:

- WhatsApp Business Cloud API integration
- Azure OpenAI integration
- Multi-language responses
- End-to-end messaging flow

## Features

- WhatsApp message reception
- Automated responses
- GPT-5 integration
- Language detection
- Basic FAQ

## Expected Outcome

Demonstrate that the platform can successfully interact with users through WhatsApp.

---

# Phase 2 — MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

## What is it?

The first version that can be used by a real gym.

## Objective

Validate the product value proposition for member loyalty.

## Features

### Customer Service

- FAQ
- Membership plans
- Opening hours
- Promotions

### Loyalty

- Welcome Journey
- Birthday messages
- Manual campaigns
- Inactive member reactivation

### Administration

- FAQ management
- Plan management
- Promotion management
- Conversation history

### Artificial Intelligence

- Multi-language support
- Knowledge base
- Conversational context

## Expected Outcome

First production deployment in a real gym environment.

---

# Phase 3 — Loyalty Advanced

## What is it?

The evolution of the loyalty platform.

## Objective

Automate journeys and increase member retention.

## Features

### Intelligent Campaigns

- Member segmentation
- Automated campaigns
- Personalized journeys

### Advanced Reactivation

- Inactive member detection
- Automated segment-based communications

### Analytics

- Retention rate
- Reactivation rate
- Engagement rate

## Expected Outcome

Reduce member churn and increase attendance frequency.

---

# Phase 4 — SaaS (Software as a Service)

## What is it?

Transformation of the solution into a multi-tenant commercial platform.

## Objective

Allow multiple gyms to use the same platform.

## Features

### Multi-Tenant

- Multiple gym management
- Data isolation

### Global Administration

- Customer management
- Subscription management
- License management

### Scalability

- Horizontal scaling
- Centralized administration

## Expected Outcome

Transform GymChat AI into a scalable commercial platform.

---

# Summary

| Phase | Objective |
|---------|-----------|
| POC | Validate technology |
| MVP | Validate the product and loyalty features |
| Loyalty Advanced | Automate engagement and retention |
| SaaS | Scale the business |
